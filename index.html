OUR VISION

To create a world where no life is lost due to a shortage of blood, ensuring timely access to safe and sufficient blood for all in need.

OUR MISSION

To inspire and empower individuals and communities to donate blood regularly, fostering a culture of kindness, care, and responsibility for saving lives.

OUR AIMS

Maintain a sustainable and reliable blood supply to meet the needs of hospitals and patients. Provide a seamless and rewarding experience for blood donors.

RECIPIENT PHASE

A patient who requires a blood transfusion to recover from illness, injury, or surgery. Their survival often depends on the availability of safe and compatible blood.

RECIPIENT PHASE

Recipients are individuals who require blood transfusions due to medical conditions, surgeries, or emergencies. These individuals may need blood for a variety of reasons.

HOSPITAL PHASE

A healthcare institution that acts as a bridge between donors and recipients. It ensures the safe storage, testing, and distribution of blood to save lives.

Go To HOSPITAL
HOSPITAL PHASE

Hospitals play a critical role in the blood donation system by collecting, testing, storing, and distributing donated blood to patients who need it.

HOSPITAL PHASE

Hospitals also manage blood inventory, match blood types between donors and recipients, and often partner with blood banks to organize donation drives and coordinate efforts it maintains.
